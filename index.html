Welcome To R.k Home Furnishing

Little Story

In 2002, R.k Home Furnishing Group carved its name in stone along the hallways of yarn spinning and carpet weaving. Today, R.k Home Furnishing provides its clients with complete floor covering solutions that include carpets, durries, rugs, bath rugs, pillows, poufs, wall hangings, storage baskets and floor cushions. Harnessing its birth from traditional design techniques, the organization has established its presence in countries across the globe.
