About Us

Welcome To R.k Home Furnishing

Aesthetically beautiful surroundings enhance happiness and contribute in overall well being of the mind and soul. We, at R.k Home Furnishing, aim to harmonize the chemistry between you and your home or office space, leaving no stone unturned to satisfy you completely. We provide an aperture to a myriad of exquisite hand as well a machine made carpets, home accessories and bath accessories to transition your surroundings into a world of class, beauty and panache.

Our products fuse meticulous craftsmanship with contemporary style. They are created with classic hand weaving techniques and rare artful accents to tell a tale that is both timeless and personal.

Our Products

The range of products made available by us is designed and manufactured as per the requirements of the industries we cater to.
